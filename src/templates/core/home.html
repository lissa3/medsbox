Compare contrast learning concept

It's not sufficient to know about a disease in an isolation.We have to understand how it compares, both in terms of clincal presentaion and mechanisms of action to other diseases that might cause the same type of symptoms or syndroms. There are some tools and methods for compare contrast learning thinking and learning.

Tools

Illness scripts - the way how experts store information about specific diseases refined by clinical experience. Each time a pt presents with a sign or symptom it triggers or activates that patricular memory store in fast and efficient way than in a random file folder.
